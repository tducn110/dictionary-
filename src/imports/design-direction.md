Design direction: make this feel designed by a creative director, not generated by AI. The key moves are typographic contrast, asymmetry, and restraint.

DO NOT change any JavaScript logic, state management, or typing mechanics.

The #1 change: introduce a serif font for the headline.
Load Google Font Playfair Display (variable, weight 400-700) via @import.
The landing page headline "your words reveal what your fingers felt" uses Playfair Display, weight 400, italic. This single choice signals "a designer made this." Sans-serif headlines = every AI landing page. Serif + italic = editorial, human, considered.
All body text, UI elements, and the typing surface stay in Inter. The serif is ONLY for the headline and section titles.

Layout: break the centered stack.
Landing page: left-align the headline to a grid. Place it at padding-left: 10vw. The subtitle goes beneath it, also left-aligned. The demo card sits to the right or spans full width below, but NOT centered with the headline. Create visual asymmetry.
Or alternatively: keep center-alignment BUT make the headline much larger (clamp(2.5rem, 7vw, 5rem)) and add a deliberate line break so the two lines have different lengths. Uneven line lengths = crafted. Even line lengths = default.

Remove ALL decorative elements:
Remove the radial gradient background overlay entirely
Remove the paper noise SVG texture
Remove the coral border on the demo card
Background: flat #F5EDE4, nothing else
Demo card: NO border, NO visible border-radius. Just white-ish background #FFFDF9 with a barely-there shadow: box-shadow: 0 1px 2px rgba(0,0,0,0.04). That's it.

Color: monochrome with ONE warm accent.
Remove the coral CTA button. Make it the same dark brown #2C2824 as before. The coral was too loud.
The ONLY color accent: the cursor blink on the writing surface and the replay progress bar — use a muted warm tone #B87A5E. Nowhere else.
Everything else is shades of warm brown/cream. No saturation.
The detail that signals craft: custom spacing and sizing.
Headline letter-spacing: -0.03em (tighter than default — this is a designer move)
Subtitle: font-size: 0.95rem, line-height: 1.7, max-width: 28ch (constraining line length shows intentional typesetting)
"FONT OF INTENT" header: letter-spacing: 0.25em, font-size: 0.7rem — smaller and more spaced than current. Quiet, not shouting.
Footer text: font-size: 0.7rem, letter-spacing: 0.15em
CTA button: remove the icon (PenLine). Just text: "Write Your Letter". Icons on buttons = template energy. A confident button is just words.

Writing surface:
Vertically center the content (justify-center on the flex column)
Remove ALL background overlays/textures. Flat #F5EDE4.
The "Finish Letter" button in the top right: text-only, no background, no border. Just the words "Finish Letter →" in warm brown, underline on hover.
The "← Back" should also be text-only, no icon. Just "back" in lowercase.

Replay view:
Dark background: #1E1B18
Text in #E8DFD4 (warm off-white, not pure white)
Progress bar: thin 1px line, filled portion in #B87A5E
Controls: minimal, just text links — "pause" / "2x" / "restart" — not icon buttons in circles

What "crafted" means: it's the ABSENCE of generic patterns.
No icons next to buttons
No pill-shaped buttons (use rounded-sm or rounded-none, not rounded-full)
No decorative gradients or textures
No centered-everything layout
Tight letter-spacing on headlines
Controlled line lengths (max-width in ch units)
One typeface pairing (serif headline + sans body), not one font for everything
